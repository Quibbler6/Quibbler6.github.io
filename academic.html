I am currently a first-year PhD student at CUHK advised by Prof. Liwei Wang. My research focus on multimodal understanding, vision-language models, and world-grounded visual intelligence. I am particularly interested in advancing visual reasoning and reliable perception in large multimodal models, especially through efficient visual representation learning, long-context video understanding, and memory-aware modeling for real-world environments.

I have also spent time in small research teams and product-minded engineering groups, where careful prototypes matter as much as clean papers.
